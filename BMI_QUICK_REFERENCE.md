## Detailed Breakdown by Category

### 🔵 UNDERWEIGHT (BMI < 18.5)

**Your Situation:**
- Low muscle mass and/or low body weight
- Need to build both mass and strength
- Often food intake is insufficient

**The Plan:**
```
Nutrition:
├─ Calories: TDEE + 400 (surplus for gains)
├─ Protein: 1.9g per kg (e.g., 133g for 70kg person)
├─ Meals: 4-5 per day (easier to hit calorie goal)
└─ Foods: Oats, rice, nuts, whole milk, eggs, chicken, dal

Training:
├─ Frequency: 4-5 days/week strength training
├─ Focus: Compound lifts (squats, deadlifts, bench, rows)
├─ Volume: Progressive overload is KEY
└─ Cardio: Minimal (only if for health/endurance)

Recovery:
├─ Sleep: 8-9 hours (critical for muscle growth)
├─ Protein timing: Post-workout (within 1 hour)
└─ Consistency: Track weight weekly
```

**Realistic Timeline:**
- Month 1: 2-4kg gain (mostly water + glycogen initially)
- Month 3: 5-8kg gain (mix of muscle + fat)
- Month 6: 10-15kg gain (depends on adherence)

**Success Metric:**
✅ Stable weight gain + increasing strength (reps/weight going up)

---

### 🟢 NORMAL WEIGHT (BMI 18.5–24.9) ✨ NEW

**Your Situation:**
- Healthy weight range
- Opportunity for true body composition change
- Can build muscle without gaining fat

**The Plan:**
```
Nutrition:
├─ Calories: TDEE (maintenance) or +200 (lean gains)
├─ Protein: 1.6g per kg (e.g., 112g for 70kg person)
├─ Macros: 30-35% protein | 40% carbs | 25-30% fats
└─ Foods: Balanced meals, prioritize quality

Training:
├─ Frequency: 4 days/week strength + 1-2 cardio
├─ Focus: Hypertrophy (muscle building)
├─ Volume: Moderate (10-20 sets per muscle group/week)
└─ Cardio: 20-30 min low-intensity or 15 min HIIT

Recovery:
├─ Sleep: 7-9 hours (for muscle recovery)
├─ Consistency: Track metrics (weight, strength, measurements)
└─ Progress: Weight stable, but strength ↑ and visuals ↑
```

**Realistic Timeline:**
- Month 1: Weight ±1kg, but body feels tighter
- Month 3: Visible muscle definition, same weight
- Month 6: 2-4kg more muscle, 2-4kg less fat (net ±0-2kg)

**Success Metric:**
✅ Recomposition: Stable weight, increased strength, visible muscle

---

### 🟡 OVERWEIGHT (BMI 25–29.9)

**Your Situation:**
- Excess body fat
- Need to lose fat while keeping muscle
- High risk of muscle loss during diet

**The Plan:**
```
Nutrition:
├─ Calories: TDEE - 400 (deficit for fat loss)
├─ Protein: 1.4g per kg (e.g., 98g for 70kg person)
├─ Prioritize: Protein first, then vegetables, then grains
└─ Foods: Chicken, fish, eggs, dal, roti, vegetables

Training:
├─ Frequency: 3-4 days/week strength (preserve muscle)
├─ Focus: Maintenance of strength (same weights/reps)
├─ Volume: Lower than muscle-building phase
└─ Cardio: 2-3x/week, 20-30 min (brisk walk/cycling)

Recovery:
├─ Sleep: 7-8 hours (especially important during deficit)
├─ Hunger management: High veggies, high protein, hydration
└─ Progress: Track weight weekly (should ↓ ~0.5kg/week)
```

**Realistic Timeline:**
- Week 4: 2kg loss
- Month 2: 3-4kg loss
- Month 3: 6-8kg loss
- **10-15 weeks to reach normal BMI** (for 15-20kg overweight)

**Success Metric:**
✅ Consistent 0.5kg/week loss + maintaining strength

---

### 🔴 OBESE (BMI ≥ 30)

**Your Situation:**
- Significant excess body fat
- Immediate health risk (metabolic, cardiovascular, joint)
- Medical supervision recommended
- Need aggressive but sustainable approach

**The Plan:**
```
Nutrition:
├─ Calories: TDEE - 500 (aggressive deficit)
├─ Protein: 1.3g per kg (e.g., 91g for 70kg person)
├─ Elimination: Sugary drinks, fried foods, processed snacks
└─ Foods: Whole foods only (chicken, fish, dal, vegetables, grains)

Training:
├─ Frequency: 2-3 days/week strength (joints need protection)
├─ Focus: Compound movements, lighter weights (form over load)
├─ Volume: Lower to protect joints
└─ Cardio: DAILY low-impact (walking 30-45 min, swimming, cycling)

Recovery & Health:
├─ Medical: Doctor checkup before exercise
├─ Sleep: 7-8 hours (critical for metabolic health)
├─ Joints: Ice after exercise if pain occurs
├─ Mental: Consistency over perfection
└─ Progress: Track weight weekly, celebrate small wins
```

**Realistic Timeline:**
- **Month 1-3:** 4-12kg loss (larger deficit, more initial water loss)
- **Month 6:** 24kg loss (6 months of 1kg/week)
- **Month 9:** Reach overweight category (BMI 25-29.9)
- **Month 12-18:** Reach normal weight (BMI 18.5-24.9)

**Success Metric:**
✅ Consistent 0.8-1.0kg/week loss + improving energy/joint health

## Calorie Adjustment Guide

### TDEE Baseline (for 70kg, 175cm, 25yo, 1.55 activity factor)
```
BMR = 1,700 kcal
TDEE = 2,635 kcal (BMR × 1.55)

Then apply goal adjustment:

Underweight:
└─ Eat: TDEE + 400 = 3,035 kcal

Normal:
├─ Maintenance: TDEE = 2,635 kcal
└─ Lean gains: TDEE + 200 = 2,835 kcal

Overweight:
└─ Fat loss: TDEE - 400 = 2,235 kcal

Obese:
└─ Aggressive loss: TDEE - 500 = 2,135 kcal
```

## Training Split Recommendations by Category

### Underweight — 5x/week Push/Pull/Legs
```
Monday: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
Tuesday: Pull (Back, Biceps)
Wednesday: REST
Thursday: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves)
Friday: Push (repeat)
Saturday: Pull (repeat)
Sunday: REST

Focus: Progressive overload, RPE 7-8 (challenging but controlled)
```

### Normal Weight — 4x/week Upper/Lower
```
Monday: Upper A (Bench, Rows, Shoulders)
Tuesday: Lower A (Squats, Deadlifts, Hamstrings)
Wednesday: REST
Thursday: Upper B (Incline, Lat Pulldowns, Accessories)
Friday: Lower B (Front Squats, Leg Press, Accessories)
Saturday/Sunday: REST + 1 cardio session (optional)

Focus: Hypertrophy (8-12 reps), RPE 7-8
```

### Overweight — 3-4x/week Full Body or PPL Lite
```
Monday: Upper (Bench, Rows, Shoulders, Biceps, Triceps)
Tuesday: Lower (Squats, Deadlifts, Legpress)
Wednesday: REST
Thursday: Accessories (Isolation work for weak points)
Friday: Optional Light Cardio
Sat/Sun: REST + daily cardio 20-30 min

Focus: Maintenance of strength, RPE 6-7 (controlled)
```

### Obese — 2-3x/week Full Body
```
Monday: Full Body (Goblet Squats, Leg Press, Chest Press, Rows)
Tuesday: REST
Wednesday: Full Body (Lateral Raises, Pulldowns, Leg Curl, Calf Raises)
Thursday-Sunday: Daily 30-45 min walking/swimming
Sat: Optional light full body

Focus: Joint safety, RPE 5-6 (easier, focus on form)
```

## Monthly Progress Checklist

### Underweight Users
- [ ] Weight ↑ by 2-4kg? (on track: +0.5-1kg/week)
- [ ] Strength ↑ by 5-10 reps or 5-10kg load?
- [ ] Eating 4-5 meals consistently?
- [ ] Protein target hit 80%+ of days?
- [ ] Energy levels high?
- [ ] Sleep 8+ hours?

### Normal Weight Users
- [ ] Weight stable ±2kg?
- [ ] Strength ↑ by 5-10 reps or 5-10kg load?
- [ ] Waist measurements ↓ or clothes fit better?
- [ ] Visible muscle definition improving?
- [ ] Completing all 4 strength sessions?
- [ ] 1-2 cardio sessions done?

### Overweight Users
- [ ] Weight ↓ by 2-3kg per month?
- [ ] Strength maintained or slightly ↑?
- [ ] Protein target hit 80%+ of days?
- [ ] Waist ↓ by 2-3cm per month?
- [ ] Energy levels good (not starving)?
- [ ] Completing 3-4 training sessions + 2-3 cardio?

## Transition Between Categories

### Underweight → Normal Weight
- Target: Gain 5-8kg to reach 23 BMI
- Timeline: 2-3 months
- Next: Transition to lean gains (TDEE + 200, 1.6g/kg protein)

### Normal Weight → Underweight
- (This is regression; avoid!)
- Should only happen if cutting too aggressively

### Normal Weight → Overweight
- (This is regression; avoid!)
- Keep tracking metrics

### Overweight → Normal Weight
- Timeline: 3-4 months (10-15kg loss at 0.5kg/week)
- Next: Transition to lean gains or maintenance

### Obese → Overweight
- Timeline: 6-9 months (~25kg loss at 1kg/week)
- Next: Transition to standard overweight plan

## Red Flags & When to Adjust

### Underweight
🚩 Not gaining for 2+ weeks → increase calories by 200
🚩 Strength not improving → increase training frequency or check protein
🚩 Feeling weak/tired → increase food quality and sleep

### Normal Weight
🚩 Weight up 3+ kg in 1 month → reduce surplus to +100
🚩 Strength stalling → increase volume or check recovery
🚩 Energy crashing → increase carbs slightly

### Overweight
🚩 Not losing for 2+ weeks → reduce calories by 100-200
🚩 Joint pain increasing → reduce cardio frequency
🚩 Feeling starving → increase low-cal veggies instead of calories

### Obese
🚩 Not losing for 2+ weeks → add extra daily walk
🚩 Joint pain → switch to swimming/cycling
🚩 Unsustainable hunger → increase protein + veggies
🚩 Any health concerns → consult doctor immediately
