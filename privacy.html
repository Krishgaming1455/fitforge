Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This page explains what information FitForge collects, how it's used, and who it's shared with. We've tried to write this in plain language rather than dense legal text.

What We Collect

DataWhy we collect it
Email addressAccount login via Supabase Auth
Name, age, weight, heightCalculate BMI, calorie/protein targets, personalise your plan
Fitness goals & experience levelAdjust your workout difficulty and recommendations
Workout history & exercise logsTrack your progress, streaks, and personal records
Food log entriesTrack daily calories, protein, and other nutrition stats
Public chat messagesPowers the Community board — visible to all other users
Private messages (DMs)Only visible to you and the person you're messaging
Public by default: Anything you post on the Community public board can be seen by any other FitForge user. Your workout routine and stats are also visible to others by default — you can hide them individually from Profile → Privacy.

Where Your Data Lives

We use Supabase as our backend database and authentication provider. Your data is stored on Supabase's servers, governed by their own privacy and security practices. We don't run our own servers for this data.

Other Services We Use

  • Supabase — account login, database storage, real-time chat delivery
  • api.qrserver.com — generates the QR code for the "Invite Friends" feature; the link you're sharing is sent to this service to produce the image
  • Google AdSense — if/when ads are enabled on this site, Google may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising via Google Ad Settings

Who Can See What

  • Public Community board — visible to everyone using the app
  • Direct Messages — only visible to you and the recipient
  • Your workout routine & stats — visible to other users by default, unless you turn on "Hide my routine" / "Hide my stats" in your profile
  • Your email, weight, height, age — always private, never shown to other users

Your Choices

  • You can use FitForge as a Guest without creating an account — guest data isn't saved long-term and disappears when you close the app
  • You can toggle your routine/stats privacy at any time from your Profile
  • You can block any user from messaging or viewing your profile via the chat menu
  • You can report any message you find inappropriate using the 🚩 icon
Account deletion: We don't currently have a self-service "delete my account" button. If you'd like your account and data removed, please contact the developer directly (see contact details below) and we'll process the request manually.

Children's Privacy

FitForge does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we'll remove it.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of FitForge after changes means you accept the updated policy.
